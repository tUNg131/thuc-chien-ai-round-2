VOICE CHARACTER GUIDELINES:

Speaker1 (Hoàng - MC, 25 years old, Male):
Hoàng is a young male Vietnamese podcast host with a warm, professional broadcaster voice. His voice should have a medium-high pitch that feels energetic yet mature, speaking at around 140 words per minute with excellent clarity and crisp consonants. The tone is consistently empathetic and caring throughout the entire podcast, like a knowledgeable friend who genuinely wants to help. His voice should carry a slight smile during greetings, slow down thoughtfully when introducing serious topics, become uplifting when offering solutions, and elevate slightly in pitch during inspirational endings. He maintains perfect Vietnamese pronunciation with accurate six-tone system and clear distinction of all diacritics (ă, â, ê, ô, ơ, ư), speaking with natural flow and strategic pauses that make listeners feel welcomed and understood. Use the Sulafat voice.

Speaker2 (Bà Ba - Guest, 72 years old, Female):
Bà Ba is a 72-year-old Vietnamese woman whose voice reflects both her age and emotional journey throughout the podcast. Her voice has a medium-low pitch with the natural breathiness and slight gravelly quality of aged vocal cords, speaking more slowly at around 110 words per minute with grandmother-like warmth. The most critical aspect is her emotional transformation: she begins weary and resigned with a tired, flat tone and low energy, progresses to frustrated and vulnerable with a tightening voice that almost breaks when describing her VNeID struggle, and finally evolves to hopeful and confident with a lighter, stronger voice quality and occasional smile by the end. Her voice should include subtle age markers like slight vocal tremor, more frequent pauses for thinking, and noticeable breath sounds, while maintaining clear Vietnamese pronunciation with a traditional Northern accent that reflects her background as a retired teacher who still articulates properly despite her age. Use the Gacrux voice.
